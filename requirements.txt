Model and Dataset Details
Model
Algorithm: Random Forest Classifier
Training: The model was trained on a dataset of employee records with features related to job satisfaction, performance, and tenure.
Input Features:
Satisfaction Level
Last Performance Rating
Number of Projects
Average Monthly Hours
Years at Company
Had Work Accident
Promoted in Last 5 Years
Department (encoded)
Salary (encoded)
Output: Binary prediction (1 for likely to leave, 0 for likely to stay).
Dataset
Source: The dataset consists of historical employee records with features related to job satisfaction, performance, and tenure.
Preprocessing: Missing values were handled with median imputation and categorical variables were encoded.
Size: Contains employee information, split into training and testing sets.
